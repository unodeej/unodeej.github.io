Welcome to Gene Block Simulator!

About this game

In Gene Block Simulator, you can live out your wildest dreams as everyone's favorite university chancellor. Manage your funds to build a thriving campus! Keep your students happy to ensure that they pay their tuition on time! Boost your school's prestige score by constructing grand buildings and managing your money well! If you run out of cash, game over!

Controls

  • Left-click on buildings to open context menus.
  • Press [F] to place buildings.
  • Press [G] to ask Gene for help.
  • Press [H] to hide Gene's advice.
